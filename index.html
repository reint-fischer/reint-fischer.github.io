Welcome

I am a Graduate Student at the Department of Atmospheric and Oceanic Science at the University of Maryland. I am fascinated by geophysical fluid dynamics, from large scale currents to mesoscale and submesoscale eddies and instabilities to small scale waves and 3D turbulence. I started my adventure in research studying Lagrangian Ocean Dynamics at Utrecht University. On this page I share my research projects, including some personal interests.
